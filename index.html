Online Dietitan & Nutritionist

I'm a Dietitian, Nutritionist and Health Coach holding a Bachelors of Health Science and Masters of Dietetics. As an Accredited Practising Dietitian, I'm qualified to give both general and medical advice, and am passionate about helping people make real, sustainable changes that will improve their daily lives. Having worked with a variety of clients in both private practice and community health, I have the expertise to help you understand nutrition and implement changes. I get so much joy and energy out of eating in a healthy, tasty and social way and I aim to help you to do the same.

I am trained to help you manage a wide range of nutrition related issues including but not limited to:


General Nutrition


○ Fitness
○ Low energy and fatigue
○ Health and wellbeing
○ Weight management
○ Plant based eating
○ Ethical and sustainable eating

Medical Nutrition


○ Food intolerances
○ Nutrient deficiencies
○ Irritable bowel syndrome
○ Type 2 diabetes
○ High blood pressure
○ High Cholesterol

Be comfortable

Have access to your kitchen

Can’t remember the name of the supplement you take? Or what type of milk you use ? When we meet online and you are at home you have easy access to your pantry, kitchen and medicine cabinet.

No, you don’t need a referral to make an appointment but you may need one to be eligible for a rebate for the session.


In order to get a rebate from medicare you need a chronic disease management plan also know as an EPC.
The current medicare rebate for dietitians is $54.60 per session
Please contact your GP to find out if you are eligible and ensure that the referral is made out to me


If you have private health insurance ‘extras cover’ you may be eligible for a rebate.
Contact your private health provider to check if you are covered and if Telehealth appointments are included
